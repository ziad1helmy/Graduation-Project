LifeLink Privacy Policy

LifeLink stores account, donation, and support data to operate donor and hospital workflows during the demo and production lifecycle.

What We Store

  • Basic account information such as name, email, and role.
  • Donation and request activity needed for matching and reporting.
  • Support submissions and notification history for operational follow-up.

How We Use It

  • To match donors with hospitals and active requests.
  • To verify accounts, support password reset, and secure access.
  • To improve reliability, auditing, and demo reporting.
