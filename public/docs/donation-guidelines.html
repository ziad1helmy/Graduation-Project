LifeLink Donation Guidelines

These guidelines are intended for demo and onboarding use. Final clinical decisions should always follow hospital screening protocols.

Before Donation

  • Bring a valid ID and confirm your registration details.
  • Drink water and eat a light meal before your appointment.
  • Do not donate if you are unwell, have a fever, or were recently advised not to donate.

During Donation

  • Follow staff instructions and report dizziness or discomfort immediately.
  • Confirm the requested blood type or donation category before proceeding.

After Donation

  • Rest briefly, hydrate, and avoid intense physical activity for the remainder of the day.
  • Contact the hospital if you feel unwell after leaving the donation site.
